Peach & Clotted Cream No-Churn Ice Cream

Why This Recipe

Peak peach season and clotted cream no-churn is the ultimate lazy summer dessert — no ice cream machine needed. Clotted cream gives an incredibly rich custardy texture that regular double cream can't match.

Ingredients

  • 200g clotted cream (chilled)
  • 395g can condensed milk, at room temperature
  • 3-4 ripe peaches, halved, stoned, and roughly chopped (~400g flesh)
  • 2 tbsp sugar (for macerating peaches)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • Optional: extra peach slices for topping, drizzle of honey

Instructions

Prep the Peach Puree

  • Toss chopped peaches with 1 tbsp sugar and lemon juice, leave for 15 minutes to macerate.
  • Blend or mash into a rough puree — keep some texture, don't go fully smooth.
  • Set aside to cool if warm from macerating.

Make the Base

  • Whip the chilled clotted cream until soft peaks form — don't over-whip, it's already thick and will turn to butter if you push it.
  • In a separate bowl, stir the condensed milk, vanilla extract, and salt until smooth.

Combine

  • Gently fold the condensed milk mixture into the whipped clotted cream — just until no white streaks remain.
  • Fold in the peach puree, but don't overmix — leave some ribbons of peach through the base for a marbled effect.

Freeze

  • Pour into a loaf tin or shallow container, press cling film directly on the surface to prevent ice crystals.
  • Freeze for at least 6 hours, preferably overnight.
  • For a proper peach swirl: dollop spoonfuls of extra peach puree on top and drag a knife through in figure-8s.

Serve

  • Let sit at room temperature for 5 minutes before scooping.
  • Top with fresh peach slices and a drizzle of honey if you have it.

Pro Tips

  • Clotted cream whips faster than double cream — check it every 10 seconds once it starts thickening. The line between 'soft peaks' and 'butter' is razor-thin.
  • If the peach puree is very runny, drain excess liquid before folding in — too much free water means icy texture.
