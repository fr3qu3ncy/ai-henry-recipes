Why These Sauces

A great dipping sauce elevates homemade pizza from "nice" to "proper thing." These range from classic to adventurous — all quick to make, all things that pair brilliantly with pizza. Pick one (or three) and keep them in a ramekin on the side.

The Sauces

Garlic Aioli
Best with: Everything. The universal dip.
  • 4 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • Splash of olive oil

Whisk everything together. Done. 2 minutes.

Chimichurri
Best with: Sausage pizza, fennel, lemon — fresh and herby
  • 1 large handful fresh parsley
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 80ml olive oil
  • 1 tsp chilli flakes
  • Pinch of salt

Blend or finely chop everything together. Let it sit 10 minutes for flavours to meld.

Hot Honey
Best with: Margherita, anything cheesy — sweet + heat
  • 3 tbsp honey
  • 1-2 tsp chilli flakes

Mix together, let sit 10 minutes. Drizzle over pizza or dip. Add more flakes if you want it hotter.

Classic Pesto
Best with: White pizza, burrata, mozzarella-heavy
  • Large handful fresh basil
  • 30g pine nuts (or walnuts)
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 40g parmesan, grated
  • 60ml olive oil

Blend until smooth, drizzle oil in slowly. Season with salt.

Intense Marinara Dip
Best with: Any pizza — double-dipping is a thing
  • 400g tin chopped tomatoes
  • 3 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Salt and black pepper

Cook down longer than you'd put on pizza — 20+ minutes until thick and intense. More garlic than you think. Cool before dipping.

Harissa Yogurt
Best with: Spiced toppings, Middle Eastern twist, slow burn
  • 100g Greek yogurt
  • 1-2 tbsp harissa paste
  • 1 tsp lemon juice

Whisk together. Creamy with a slow-building heat. Surprisingly brilliant with pizza.

Truffle Aioli
Best with: Mushrooms, truffle oil pizza, fancy nights
  • 4 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp truffle oil
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed

Three ingredients. Mix together. Fancy but effortless.

Balsamic Glaze
Best with: Burrata, fresh tomatoes, anything cheesy
  • Good quality balsamic vinegar reduction

Buy it or reduce balsamic vinegar in a pan until syrupy. Thick, sweet-tart. You probably already have this from the peach salad.

Buffalo Sauce
Best with: Chicken pizza, American-style, bold flavours
  • 3 tbsp Frank's RedHot (or hot sauce)
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • Splash of white vinegar

Mix together. Classic American heat.

Sriracha Mayo
Best with: Asian-inspired toppings, quick creamy kick
  • 4 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1-2 tbsp sriracha
  • Squeeze of lime

Whisk together. Adjust sriracha to your heat preference.
