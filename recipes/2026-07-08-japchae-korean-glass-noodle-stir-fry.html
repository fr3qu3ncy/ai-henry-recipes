Japchae (Korean Glass Noodle Stir Fry)

Why This Recipe

Korean glass noodles tossed with beef, colorful vegetables, and a sweet-savory sesame-soy dressing — it's sort of a stir fry, sort of a noodle salad, served barely warm. Perfect summer dish that's vibrant and comes together in 35 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 300g/10oz boneless beef short ribs (or scotch fillet/boneless rib eye, tenderised)
  • 2 tsp soy sauce (for marinade)
  • 2 tsp white sugar (for marinade)
  • 2 tsp finely minced garlic (for marinade)
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper (for marinade)
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce (for sauce)
  • 2 tsp white sugar (for sauce)
  • 1/2 tsp finely minced garlic (for sauce)
  • 1 1/2 tbsp sesame oil (for sauce)
  • 1/2 tsp salt (for sauce)
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper (for sauce)
  • 250g/8oz sweet potato noodles (dangmyeon), dried
  • 4 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp salt, divided
  • 1 onion, peeled, halved, sliced into 6mm wedges
  • 3 green onion stems, cut into 5cm lengths, white & green parts separated
  • 200g/7oz fresh shiitake mushrooms, stems removed, sliced 5mm thick
  • 2 carrots, peeled, cut into 3mm batons
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into 0.5cm slices
  • 4 1/2 cups (tightly packed) baby spinach
  • 2 tbsp white sesame seeds

Instructions

Prep

  • Cut beef into strips ~5mm thick, 1cm wide, 5cm long. If using short ribs, trim excess fat, cut meat off bone, then slice into 5mm thick strips.
  • Marinate: Put beef in a bowl, add marinade ingredients (2 tsp soy sauce, 2 tsp sugar, 2 tsp minced garlic, 1/2 tsp black pepper). Mix well. Set aside for 15-20 minutes.
  • Sauce: Mix the sauce ingredients (1/4 cup soy sauce, 2 tsp sugar, 1/2 tsp minced garlic, 1 1/2 tbsp sesame oil, 1/2 tsp salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper) in a very large mixing bowl.
  • Noodles: Cook sweet potato noodles per packet directions (normally 8 minutes in boiling water). Drain, briefly rinse under tap water, then shake off excess water well. Add noodles into the bowl with the dressing. DO NOT MIX yet.

Cook Vegetables

  • Batch 1: Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a large non-stick pan (30cm/12") over high heat. Cook the onion, white parts of green onion, and shiitake mushrooms with 1/2 tsp salt for 2 1/2 to 3 minutes, stirring constantly, just until starting to soften but not going golden. Pour on top of the noodles — don't mix!
  • Batch 2: Using the same pan still on high heat, heat 1 tbsp oil then cook the carrots and bell pepper for 1 1/2 minutes, constantly stirring. Add spinach, green onion green parts, and the remaining 1/2 tsp salt. Keep cooking for 1 1/2 minutes until the spinach is wilted. Transfer into the noodle bowl — still don't mix!

Cook Beef

  • In the same pan, heat the final 1 tbsp of oil still on high heat. Add beef and cook for 1 1/2 minutes until very lightly golden and just cooked through. Add to the noodle bowl. Don't mix!

Combine & Serve

  • Add most of the sesame seeds (reserve some for topping). NOW mix everything — toss, toss, toss until well combined.
  • Transfer into a serving bowl, sprinkle with remaining sesame seeds. Serve warm, not piping hot.

Pro Tips

  • Sweet potato noodles (dangmyeon) are available in large grocery stores and Korean/Asian markets. If you can't find them, substitute with glass noodles (bean thread noodles) or vermicelli.
  • The key technique is cooking everything separately then combining in the bowl — this keeps each ingredient at its perfect texture. Don't skip the 'don't mix yet' steps!
